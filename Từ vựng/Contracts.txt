- Abide (v) by
+ Definition: Tuân thủ/ tuân theo
+ Example: The two parties agreed to abide by the judge's descision

 - Agreement(n)
+ Definition: Sự thỏa thuận (k đếm được), = contract = hợp đồng (đếm được) 
+ Example: According to the agreement, the caterer will also supply the flowers for the event

- Assurance (n) - Assure (v)
+ Definition: Sự đảm bảo - đảm bảo
+ Example: The sales associate gave his assurance that the missing keyboard would be replaced the next day.

-  Cancellation (n) - cancel (v)
+ Definition: Sự hủy bỏ - chấm dứt
+ Exam: The cancellation clause appears at the back of the contract

- Determine (v)
+ Definition: xác định
+ Exam: After reading the contract, I was unable to determine if our company was liable for back wages
+ liable for : có trách nhiệm/ nghĩa vụ cho....

- Engage (v)
+ Definition: tham dự
+ Exam: He engaged us in a fascinating discussion about current bussiness law.
+ fascinating(a): lôi cuốn/ hấp dẫn

- Establish (v)
+ Definition: Thành lập/ thiết lập
+ Exam: The merger of the two companies established a powerful new corporation:
+ corporation(n): tập đoàn

- Obligate (v)
+ Definition: Bắt buộc/ràng buộc
+ Exam: I felt obligated to finish the project even though I could have exercised my option to quit.

- Party (n): 
+ Definition: Bên (tham gia hợp đồng)
+ Exam: the parties agreed to settlement in their contract dispute
+ settlement: hướng giải quyết/ sự giải quyết
+ dispute: tranh chấp

- Provision (n)
+ Definition: Sự chu cấp/cung cấp hoặc Điều khoản
+ Exam: The father made provisions for his children through his will

- Resolve (v)
+ Definition: Giải quyết/ quyết định
+ Exam: The bussinessman resolved to clean out all the files by the end of the week

- Specific (adj)
+ Definition: cụ thể
+ Exam: In the contract, one specific word can change the meaning dramatically.


Note: 
+ Representative trông như tính từ nhưng thực chất là danh từ (người đại diện)
+ [time] + in advance: trước bao lâu đó...
+ give(v) sb notice: thông báo cho ai đó. 
+ Terminate = cancel: hủy bỏ, phá vỡ
+ specification(n): Chi tiết kĩ thuật/ sự chỉ rõ.
+ Specify(v): chỉ rõ
+ calls for: yêu cầu/kêu gọi
+ Bargaining: sự thương lượng.
