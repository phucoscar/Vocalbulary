Affordable (adj):
- Phải chăng, vừa phải, hợp lý (nói về giá cả) = reasonable
- Exam: They are meeting consumer demand for fashionable products at affordable/resonable prices.


As needed (adv)
As - needed (adj)
- Khi cần thiết
- Exam: The courier service did not come every day, only as needed.
	The service contract states that repairs will be made on an as - needed basis.
- state: trạng thái or nêu lên rằng
- on an as - needed basis: trên cơ sở cần thiết.


In charge of (pre, phrase - giới từ)
- Có trách nhiệm, có bổn phận.
- Exam: He appointed someone to be in charge of maintaining a supply of paper in the fax machine.
- appoint: bổ nhiệm.
- appoinment: cuộc hẹn.


Capacity(n):
- Sức chứa.
- Năng lực (= ability)
- Exam: The stadium has a seating capacity of 50000.
	Do you think it's within his capacity to do the job without making any a mess of it.
- mess: sự xáo trộn.


Durable (adj):
- Bền, bền bỉ.
- Exam: The machines have to be made of durable materials.


Initiative (n):
- Sáng kiến
- Sự chủ động (cụm thường gặp: take the initiative)
- Exam: Joe Walker will be spearheading our new marketing initiative.
	Employees are encouraged to take the initiative and share their ideas with management.
- Spearheading (v): mũi nhọn, đứng đầu.


Physically (adv):
- Về thân thể, thể chất, dáng vẻ bên ngoài.
- Exam: The computer screen is making her physically sick.


Provider (n):
- Nhà cung cấp (= supplier)
- Exam: This company has become the major internet service provider.


Recur (v):
- Lặp lại, tái diễn.
- Exam: The manager did not want that particular error to recur.
- Occur(v): xảy ra.
- particular (adj): cụ thể.

Reduction (n):
- Sự sụt giảm ( + in)
- Exam: The cutbacks have required a 10% reduction in staff numbers.
- cutback (n): sự cắt giảm.


Stay on top of (phrase):
- Cập nhật thông tin, diễn biến mới nhất.
- Exam: In this industry, you must stay on top of current developments.


Stock (n, v):
- Kho dự trữ, hàng hóa trong kho, sự tích trữ.
- Tích trữ
- In stock: còn hàng, out of stock: hết hàng.
- Exam: The employees stocked the shelves on a weekly basis.
	The office's stock of toner for fax machine was quickly running out.
- running out (v): cạn kiệt, hết.
