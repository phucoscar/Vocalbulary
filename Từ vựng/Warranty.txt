Warranty (n):
- Sự bảo đảm, bảo hành

Characteristic (adj, n):
- Đặc điểm, đặc trưng, tính năng.
- Exam: Once characteristic of the store is that it is slow in mailing refund check.


Consequence (n):
- Hậu quả, kết quả
- Exam: The consequence of not following the service instructions for your car is that the warranty is invalidated.
+ instruction: hướng dẫn
+ invalidated: vô hiệu hóa

Consider(v):
- Xem xét
- Exam: After considering all the options, Della decided to buy a used car

Cover(v):
- Bao quát, bảo vệ, che trở
- (đủ tiền) chi trả
- Exam: Will my medical insurance cover this surgery
+ insurance (n): Bảo hiểm
+ surgery (n): phẫu thuật

Expiration (n):
- Hạn sử dụng, hết hạn
- Exam: Have you checked the expiration date on this yogurt?

Imply (v):
- Ngụ ý, ám chỉ.
- Exam: The guarantee on the iphone implied that all damages were coverd under warranty for one year.

Promise (v; n):
- Hứa; lời hứa/cam kết.
- Exam: A warranty is a promise the manufacturer makes to the consumer.

Protect (v):
- Bảo vệ
- Exam: You can protect yourself from scams by getting detailed information on the seller.
+ Cú pháp: protect sb from sth

Reputation (n):
- Danh tiếng, uy tín
- Exam: The company knew that the reputation of its products was the most important asset it had.
+ asset (n): tài sản


Variety (n):
- Sự đa dạng
- Exam: There's a variety of terms that you'll find in warranties.
+ cụm: a variety of: sự đa dạng về cái gì đó.
