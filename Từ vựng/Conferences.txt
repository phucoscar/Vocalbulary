Conferences: hội nghị

Accommodate (v): 
- Cung cấp (chỗ ở); đáp ứng.
- Exam: The meeting room is large enough to accommodate the various needs of the group using it.


Arrangement (n):
- Sự sắp xếp
- Exam: The travel arrangement were taken care of by Sara, Mr Billing's capable assistant.
- capable (adj): có khả năng, có năng lực.

Association (n):
- Sự liên kết
- Hiệp hội; tổ chức
- Exam: Membership in a profressional association provides business contacts and mutual support.
- mutual (adj): qua lại.


Attend (v):
- Tham dự
- Exam: We expect more than 100 members to attend the annual meeting.
- Attendance (n): Sự tham dự/ người tham dự.


Get in touch (v):
- liên lạc.
- Exam: As soon as we arrive at the hotel, we will get in touch with the manager about the unexpected guests.


Hold (v):
- Cầm, giữ
- Tổ chức (cuộc họp, hội nghị, sự kiện,...)
- Chứa đựng = Accommodate
- Exam: She holds an annual seminar on Tuesday.
	This meeting room holds at least 80 people 	comfortably.

Overcrowded (adj):
- Quá Đông đúc
- Exam: To avoid being overcrowded, we limited the number of guests that members could bring.


Session (n):
- buổi (họp, huấn luyện,...)/ phiên.
- Exam: The morning sessions tend to finish earlier than expected
- tend (v): có xu hướng.



Take part in = Participate in: tham gia vào...
