Attract(v): Thu hút
+ Exam: The display attracted a number of people at the convention.
+ convention: trung tâm, hội nghị

Compare(v): so sánh, xem xét.
+ Exam:One the customer compared the two products, her choice was easy.

Competition(n): Cuộc thi, sự cạnh tranh
+Exam: The company has decided not to join the growing competition for dominance in the semiconductor market.
+ dominance(n): sự thống trị (thường dùng cho phạm vi phổ biến hơn)
+ domination (n): sự thống trị (thường dùng cho phạm vi chính trị)
+ dominate (v): thống trị
+ semiconductor: link kiện bán dẫn

Consume(v): Tiêu thụ, sử dụng.
+ Exam: This printer consumes more toner than the downstairs printer
+ toner: mực


Convince(v): thuyết phục
+ Exam: the salesman conviced his customer to buy his entire inventory of pen
+ entire: toàn bộ
+ inventory: tồn kho

Inspiration (n): Nguồn cảm hứng
+ Exam: His work is an inspiration to the marketing department
+ department: phòng ban

Market (n): thị trường
+ Exam: The market for brightly colored clothing was brisk last year, but it's moving sluggishly this year.
+ brisk: sôi nổi, tăng nhanh
+ sluggish: chậm chạp, trì trệ

Persuasion(n): Sự thuyết phục = convince
+ Exam: Under his persuasion, she returned to school for her MBA

Productive(adj): Năng suất
+ Exam: Alonzo is excited about his productive staff.

Satisfaction(n): sự hài lòng
+ Exam: Your satisfaction is guaranteed or you'll get your money back.
+ guarantee: đảm bảo, bảo hành
+ satisfactory(adj): thỏa đáng
